## Full Description (4000 chars max)

CannaRoute Driver is the delivery partner app for licensed cannabis couriers working with dispensaries on the CannaRoute platform.

**Accept Runs Instantly**
See incoming delivery requests in real time. Accept or decline with one tap. View the order details, destination, and estimated payout before you commit.

**Navigate with Confidence**
Integrated routing guides you through every delivery efficiently. Customer addresses are only revealed after you accept a run — no browsing addresses without an active delivery.

**Live GPS Sharing**
During active deliveries, your location is shared with the customer so they can track their order in real time. GPS broadcasting stops the moment delivery is confirmed.

**Proof of Delivery**
Capture a delivery photo or collect a digital signature at drop-off. Every delivery is logged with timestamp, location, and confirmation — giving you and the dispensary a complete chain of custody.

**Earnings Dashboard**
Track completed deliveries, earnings per run, and weekly totals from your Account screen.

**Compliance Built In**
CannaRoute Driver enforces age verification at delivery. Customers must show valid ID — the app walks you through the verification checklist. You never need to guess the protocol.

---

*This app is for licensed delivery couriers working with CannaRoute partner dispensaries. Drivers must complete background screening and onboarding before first delivery.*
