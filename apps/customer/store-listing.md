## Full Description (4000 chars max)

CannaRoute connects adults 21+ with licensed cannabis dispensaries for on-demand delivery — all through a clean, fast, and fully compliant mobile experience.

**Browse Verified Dispensaries**
Discover licensed dispensaries near you with live menus, real-time inventory, and verified product information. Every dispensary on CannaRoute is licensed and compliant in their operating state.

**Shop with Confidence**
Browse flower, concentrates, edibles, and more with full product transparency — sourced directly from licensed growers. See lab results, strain details, and potency information before you buy.

**Track Your Order Live**
Follow your delivery from confirmation to door. Watch your driver on a live map and receive push notifications at every step — placed, confirmed, picked up, delivered.

**Age-Verified & Compliant**
CannaRoute operates only in states where cannabis delivery is legal. All users are age-verified at account creation and again at delivery. We never ship across state lines.

**Pay Your Way**
Multiple payment options supported. All transactions are compliant with applicable state regulations.

**CannaRoute is available in licensed delivery states only. You must be 21 or older to use this app. Medical patients must have a valid medical card on file.**
